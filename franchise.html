OWNED BY FRANCHISES

In a market dominated by global giants like McDonald’s, Domino’s, KFC, and Burger King—each earning over ₹15,000 crore
annually from India—options for homegrown alternatives were scarce. But in 2011, Biraja Rout, a visionary from Bangalore,
set up a humble kiosk serving grilled burgers. While the concept was familiar, two things set it apart: it was founded by
an Indian, and the patties were grilled, not fried. This wasn’t just another burger joint—it was the beginning of India’s
very own burger brand, built for the future.
